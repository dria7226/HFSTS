UNDERFLOW
 0 - no underflow
 1 - underflow

DIVISION_BY_ZERO
 0 - no division by zero
 1 - division by zero

 0 - hasn't failed
 1 - failed and capacity is invalid
 2 - failed and was unable to allocate memory
